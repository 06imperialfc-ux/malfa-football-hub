# Project Scope

## Visual direction

The platform has been redesigned as an association website rather than a club website. It uses the MALFA red, graphite, white and neutral grey palette, editorial sports typography, squared controls and a separate light/dark appearance system.

Typography is limited to two sans-serif families:

- Archivo for display and headings
- Inter for body text and interface controls

## Included divisions

U11, U13, U15, U17, U19, MPL, Super League and WPL.

## Removed

Player leaderboards, top scorers, assists and clean sheets are not included.

## Recommended content before launch

- Final club list and approved crests
- Official league rules and promotion/relegation markers
- Current fixtures and venues
- Association contact information and social links
- Sponsor and partner logos
- Privacy policy and website terms
